Swagat

After a month-long exercise and wanderings through the maze of counselling for admission in one of the most prestigious NITs of the country, the newly admitted batch of first year students of undergraduate and postgraduate programmes at Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology (MNNIT), Allahabad witnesses the annual freshers’ function “Swagat” at the sprawling Rajiv Gandhi Multi-Purpose Hall.
This is very first event of college when you start your college life here at MNNIT. It is an event which acts as a platform for the newly inducted students to exhibit their extra-curricular talents. Besides it also facilitates better co-ordination and communication between the freshers and the senior students. It is purely an event dedicated to students, hence right from the selection of the events to the management of the function, the responsibility is being borne by senior students in the form of a committee of student co-ordinators.
There are 14 committees that make this event a grand success. This ranges from dainty dances like Garba, Marathi, Bengali, SouthFolk, Bhangra, Rajasthani, Inaugural and Western to the charming ramp walks of the footprints committee and from the extraordinary talent of the dramatics committee to the technical upbringing of Media House of MNNIT. The Arts committee brightens the stage with a outstanding back drop painted by vibrant colours. The Music committee which sets the stage on fire with their melodious and enchanting performances. The Oration committee which lefts the audience awestruck with their affluent skills.

Technical Fest- Avishkar
-Ideate Innovate and Create

Avishkar, MNNIT is one of the biggest Science and Technology Festival. It came into existence, with the motto of promoting science and technology and to foster genuine interest in students to come up with technological solutions to real life problems. Techfest has surpassed many years of creating an unmatched aura of a science and technology spectacle year after year. It’s captivating night shows, famous artists and fun setups keeps the festive spirit alive throughout the 4 days of the festival.
Having the theme ‘ideate, innovate and create’, the fest has around 60 events being organised over the period of four days. There are over 12 major banners under which whole Avishkar takes place into account. It includes Aerodynamics, Cyberquest, Mechrocosm, Electromania, Power Surge, Genesis, Rasayans, Nirman, Oligopoly, Kreedomania, Monopoly and Robomania.
Every year Avishkar occurs at mid-September which witnesses a huge participation from all branches and years. Avishkar’ 19 marked a huge success all it had a team of dedicated students, who worked day and night passionately. From self-driving cars which can follow lanes and identify road signals to creating ‘Vikram’ lander inspired ATVs for different types of terrains on moon, form google assistant based home automation to creating your own android app Avishkar keeps on raising the bar year after year.
There are two major sub-events that are held during 4 days of Avishkar which are as follows:

GNOSIOMANIA

This is one of the most prominent and the distinguished quiz fests of the country where prominent quizmasters such as Somnath Chanda, Adityanath Mubai, Major Chandrakant Nair and Kushan patel conducts quizzes on the genres like General Quiz, Maha Quiz, Sports Quiz , Mela Quiz etc with the quizzers from all over India.
Gnosiomania is thus 2-day grand celebration of gyan, fundae and human ingenuity.

GNO TALKS

Another event which occurs during Avishkar is Gno Talks. The GnoTalks is a unique conceptualised talk show where celebrities from various walks of life perform their art and inspire the budding techies and managers with their stories and struggles.
GnoTalks has emerged from an open panel discussion to people of great intellect inspiring young minds and leaving them in awe.
Motto of Gno Talks "Where Actions Speak" clearly states the ideology and the need of this eve. Speakers from various fields present not to just speak but to interact with the audience. For a brief moment in history, this is zenith of human intellect and abilities.
MNNIT had a very profound panel for gnoTalks which includes Param Vir Chakra winner Subedar Major Yogendra Singh Yadav, The Robin Hood Army, TVF, stand up comedian Rahul Subramaniam, Chetan Bhagat, former captian of Indian football team I.M. Vijayan, , co-founder of Super 30 Abhayanand, one of the finest archers in India Sanjeev Kumar Singh, speed and sand painter and sculptor Sarvam Patel and the list goes on and on.

CULRAV

Initiated in 1987, Culrav has emerged as the most resplendent college-based fest in Northern India and as one of the most awaited cultural event of the country. Culrav have breath taking competitions, glamorous pro-nites and appealing informal events due to which Culrav has attracted humongous footfall from colleges all over the country. The likes of "Farhan Akhtar, "Piyush Mishra " and "Vinay Pathak" have graced Culrav’s stage along with exhilarating performances from “The Local Train”,” Shirley Sethia”, "DJ Zaeden" and many more.
There are a lot of events that occurs during the fest such as : Razzmatazz where various dance teams from different colleges compete to win the title being judged by the top dancers every year.
Rangmanch and Parimal, an umbrella event for all Hindi literary events,
Kavya Sandhya, the inaugural flagship poetry night of Culrav which attracts connoisseurs of poetry, showcasing their talent judged by eminent personalities.
Celebrity and Pro-nites the last two nights of Culrav witnesses thrilling performances from famous celebrities and artists in the fields of rock, hip-hop or EDM.
Spandan which witnesses the charming glamourous ramp walks.
Informal Events from "Stand-up Comedy" to "Googly Cricket" it has a wide variety of events in this arena, to enthral the audiences all the time wherein they stand a chance to win lots of goodies and merchandise.
Stunt Show being hosted by Hero Moto Corp has gathered huge amount of audience last year.
Culrav in its all glory is a story of the zeal and spontaneity that MNNIT holds within its campus – in its students and to support them, their faculty co-ordinators, mentors and staff.

Prosang as a word is a portmanteau of Prodyogiki and Sangam.

ROTARACT CLUB

Rotaract is a network of friendship engagement and action that spans 167 countries. Rotaract clubs are part of a global effort to bring peace and international understanding to the world. The purpose of Rotaract is to provide an opportunity for young men and women to enhance the knowledge and skills to address physical and social needs of the communities. Rotaract Club is one of the most prominent clubs of MNNIT. The members of this club teach the underprivileged students of Bal Bharti School under the initiative “DISHA”.

Visiting Blind Schools and Old Age Homes and carrying out Blood Donation Camp are some of the main initiatives taken by this club.
