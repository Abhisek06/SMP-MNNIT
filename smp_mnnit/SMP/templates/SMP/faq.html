Do I need to fear seniors from being ragged?

This is one of the most common questions but it is absolutely a NO. You can find seniors more friendly than you usually expect. They give you treats. They give you better advice, either it be in academics or about your passion.

Is there any option for branch sliding?

Yes,you can apply for branch sliding at the end of first year.But the condition for eligibility is that you have to score cpi at least 7.5.The applicant must have passed all credits of the 1st year of the programme in a single attempt. Strength of any programme will not exceed its sanctioned strength or fall below 60% of its sanctioned strength as a result of the changes. Different cutoff are declared for different branches

What to bring from home?

There is a general purpose shop in the first year’s hostel. You can have things of basic necessities from there like drafter, copies, flip-flops, bucket, soap, shampoo, chips, pens, etc.

You can have a pair of formals for the Avishkar interviews (Tech fest of college),a party-wear dress for Swagat(Fresher’s party).

What all extra activities will I have the exposure to, for personality development?

Once you reach here at MNNIT you will be provided with ample of opportunities in the face of a number of clubs and committees for which you help you identify your talents and your strength and thus work upon them to leave no stone unturned. ... Talking about the various committees of our college from the cultural perspective we have: Dance committee (which is further divided into other subcategories), Drams committee (which is also further divided into other subcategories), MHM (Media House of MNNIT), Music Committee, Arts Committee, Oration Committee, Footprints. Now talking from extra co-curricular point of view we have: Student Mentorship Program, Gnosis Quiz Club, Computer Coding Club (CC Club), Robotics Club, Aero Club, Astronomy Club, E- Cell (Entrepreneurship -Cell), Literary Club. If you are interested in sports then we have MNNIT Athletic Club, MNNIT Football Club, MNNIT Cricket Club, Table Tennis, Lawn Tennis, Volleyball, Basketball, Badminton, Hockey, Judo-Karate, Skating facilities equipped with coaching staff available for you. For first year students the entry to the cultural committees can be either through Swagat Auditions (Official Fresher’s) or after Swagat as per the audition conducted by respective committee/club. Entry to the technical clubs is mostly open post Swagat time and will be done through the interviews and that for Sports Club is subjected to the trials. All in all here at MNNIT you will witness sea of opportunities, all you have to do is just have faith in yourself and discover and develop yourself.
