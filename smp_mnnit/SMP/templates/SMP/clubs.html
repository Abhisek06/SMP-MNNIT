MNNIT Literary Club

MNNIT, Literary club is a club where students discover their hidden literary talents and step out of their shell. A club which unhides your creativity and allows you to showcase your talent in best way possible. The club provide ample of opportunities to writers, orators and debaters. From MUN, parliamentary debate, MIP to poetry slam, storytelling, the club organises enormous such activities throughout the year. Literary club has proved itself in many IITs and NITs. This club played a major role in bagging up overall championship trophy in SPRINGFEST 2019 and 2020(Cultural fest of IIT KGP).

Gnosis Quiz Club

Gnosis derived from Ancient Greek, translates to having special knowledge of spiritual mysteries. The Quiz club, founded in 2011, is one of the most active clubs of the institute with a plethora of quizzes covering enormous varieties of genres. The club organises SAC quizzes once in a week. The club also organises ‘Gnosiomania’, the quizzing event held during Avishkar (the tech-fest of MNNIT) which hosts quizmasters and quizzers from all over India. Many of the alumnus who were once the part of this club have made MNNIT proud at National Level Quizzing like Tata Crucible Campus Quiz, SBI Numero YONO Quiz etc. The genres of quizzes range from Mela, General, India, Bollywood, Sports to Sci-Biz Tech, pop-culture and many more. The genres of quizzes seem to stretch far and thus, no stones are left unturned.

Robotics Club

If you have ever even secretly aspired to build your own robot, then the Robotics Club, MNNIT is the place for you! MNNIT has diverse group of over-enthused robotics nerds who find roots across all academic departments of the institute. The club is a student body which finds its place under the Science and Technology Council. It provides one on one guidance, workshops and tutorials along with tools, equipments, components and workspaces which eagerly await you.

Robotics Club MNNIT was formed out of the passion for robotics, to facilitate learning and research in the areas of Mechatronics/robotics and Intelligent Systems, Automation, Advanced Control Systems, Modelling and Simulation, and Sensors & Algorithms. This aims at acquiring knowledge and appropriate hands-on experience, in order to meet the needs of these rapidly changing technologies and provide services to industry for promoting new technologies as well as designing and manufacturing commercially viable products, for the development of our country.

Robotics club also hosts multiple events in Avishkar such as Daksha, Mind the path and Mind the traffic.

Anokhi Pehel

Anokhi Pehel is a non-profitable organisation initiated by the group of students who share common motive of helping underprivileged students. This club is initiated by Kanad Kr. Amar and his friends (2020 batch). Three years back, they came up with the idea of “each one teach one”. And laid the foundation of this club which has now more than 400 members. A large number of students from all courses and streams are volunteering to teach the less privileged students. The managing and teaching team of the organisation is aimed at bringing students from their homes, teaching them in evening and then dropping them back at their homes. This club organises “Antyodaya” (annual celebratory fest) in which these students participate in different competitions of cultural, academic and sports interest to build their personality as a whole.

Green Club

MNNIT Green Club is founded by Ashish Jaiswal and his friends (2021 batch). The club was inaugurated by the honourable president of India, Ramnath Kovind Ji on 15 Dec 2017. They planted around 1000 plants all over the campus on different occasions. Till now, nearly 3000 planted have been planted by this club. During summer times, they arranged almost 200 water pots for birds all across the campus, under the supervision of official in-charge.

As a part of Green Club initiative, students are now advised to bring water bottles during the exam. In addition, instead of plastic cups, recyclable paper cups are being used during the exam time to stop the usage of plastic in the campus.

This club also organises cleanliness drive to tell students of an institute of national importance that, “If we cannot keep our own college clean then are, we really educated?”.

Rotaract Club

Rotaract is a network of friendship engagement and action that spans 167 countries. Rotaract clubs are part of a global effort to bring peace and international understanding to the world. The purpose of Rotaract is to provide an opportunity for young men and women to enhance the knowledge and skills to address physical and social needs of the communities. Rotaract Club is one of the most prominent clubs of MNNIT. The members of this club teach the underprivileged students of Bal Bharti School under the initiative “DISHA”.

Extracurricular activities are also being organised which helps to overall development of these students.

Cloth Donation Drives are also an important initiative carried out by this club.

They collect blankets, woollen clothes from the college students and nearby areas and distribute them to needy in winters.

Visiting Blind Schools and Old Age Homes and carrying out Blood Donation Camp are some of the main initiatives taken by this club. Aiming at making this society a better place to live in, Rotaract Club becomes one of the major attractions of MNNIT.

MNNIT SAE Club

Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) is the global society for all the automobile engineering enthusiasts. As MNNIT is a part of its collegiate society, it participates in all the three mega events: SAE-BAGA, a national level event design; SAE-SUPRA, a self-made car racing event; and SAE-EFFICYCLE, the event for three wheeled electric or self-paddled tricycle. A green bike made by MNNIT students has hit the campus road which uses the e-bike recovery system. The students have also made a GO-KART for an event in March 2020.

MSME Club

MNNIT Society of mechanical engineers (MSME) is the club run by final year students of Mechanical and Production and Industrial engineering branch. It provides extra classes to hone the soft skills for the first, second- and third-year students along with preparing for the doom’s day with mock GD’s and mock interviews. ENACTUS

Enactus works with a vision to create a better, more sustainable world. Enactus MNNIT is the student chapter of Enactus at Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology, Allahabad. We believe that every individual can make an impact in their community by using the positive power of entrepreneurship.

Enactus MNNIT Allahabad is a committee of registered students who work along the guidelines of ENACTUS - a non-profit international NGO constituting 36 nations globally, with a prime objective of improving the standards of living across the world. We recognize the abundant human skill in the underprivileged section of our society and work upon ways to develop business model to help them become self-sufficient in terms of income and overcome the stigma of social discrimination. This club conduct various projects based on numerous surveys and countless researches which tackles various problems in accordance to the 17 sustainable development goals designed by the united nations leaders for better world.

Astronomy Club

The curiosity of the students regarding the universe are taken care by this club. The main aim of the club is to promote amateur astronomy and popularise it in the institute. Frequent lectures and talks by students, faculty and other renowned institutions are organised by the members for the campus community.

This club is group of 10 people from various years of the college exploring various theoretical and experimental aspects of Astronomy. Some of the major projects this club is working upon include 3D printing a telescope, Solar system and voyager trajectory simulation in MATLAB, Radio Astronomy at RAD@home, Processing of planetary image data, sky objects cataloguing and the list goes on and on.

This club welcomes all those students who perceive this as a science, hobby or a sport. Objective of it is to unify these like-minded enthusiasts on a common platform and do something exciting in new ways.

Music Club

Nishaad, the music club of MNNIT Allahabad. The club had very humble beginnings when only people who were part of the club used to participate. As time slowly progressed, it soon became one of the fastest growing clubs in MNNIT with huge participation and audience for the events. The club promotes good music and the seniors hone the talents of their juniors. The music club is at the forefront of every college event from Culrav to Republic day gathering. They have invited bands like The Local Train, Parvaaz, Chronic Xorn to perform in the college.

Apart from this, the club has a plethora of achievements under its belt. Every year members of the club represent it in major cultural fests across India, Springfest at IIT KGP, Antaragini at IIT Kanpur, Effervescence at IIIT Allahabad, Kaashiyatra at IIT BHU and Mood Indigo at IIT Mumbai. The club members have secured pole positions at these fests.
