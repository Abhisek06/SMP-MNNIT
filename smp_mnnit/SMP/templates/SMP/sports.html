About Sports

Excellence in sports is a direct index of the stature and intellectual maturity of an institute. MNNIT has always aimed to set a precedent for other institutions by laying utmost emphasis on the multifaceted development of its students and pushing them to raise the bar by striving to be ‘Citius, Altius, Fortius’.All the facilities are helmed by seasoned training staff so that even beginners have a beautiful chance to learn the craft and their more experienced counterparts can receive the requisite guidance.

You are here for an overall development of your personality, so to keep you healthy and fit, MNNIT has all the facilities for sports, both indoor and outdoor.

All outdoor sports like athletics, cricket, football, hockey, basketball, volleyball, etc. and indoor sports like table tennis, weight lifting, chess, carrom etc. are actively played by all throughout the year. Institute organize and promote all extra-curricular activities in the field of sports. It has currently twelve clubs:

  • Athletics Club
  • Badminton Club
  • Basketball Club
  • Cricket Club
  • Football Club
  • Hockey Club
  • Lawn Tennis Club
  • Table Tennis Club
  • Volleyball Club
  • Skating Club
  • Taekwondo Club
  • Karate Club

Athletics Meet

Athletics Meet, under Student Activity Centre is a competition held every winter at MNNIT. Being a three-day event, there more than 80 competitions covering all formal and informal events. The Meet affords the opportunity to the sportspersons from the institute to showcase their talents.

So, each one of you do make it a point to take some time out from your busy schedules for sports and extra-curricular activities.
