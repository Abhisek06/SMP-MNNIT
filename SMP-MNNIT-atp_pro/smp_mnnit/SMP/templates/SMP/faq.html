Do I need to fear seniors from being ragged?

This is one of the most common questions but it is absolutely a NO. You can find seniors more friendly than you usually expect. They give you treats. They give you better advice, either it be in academics or about your passion.

Is there any option for branch sliding?

Yes,you can apply for branch sliding at the end of first year.But the condition for eligibility is that you have to score cpi at least 7.5.The applicant must have passed all credits of the 1st year of the programme in a single attempt. Strength of any programme will not exceed its sanctioned strength or fall below 60% of its sanctioned strength as a result of the changes. Different cutoff are declared for different branches

What to bring from home?

There is a general purpose shop in the first year’s hostel. You can have things of basic necessities from there like drafter, copies, flip-flops, bucket, soap, shampoo, chips, pens, etc.

You can have a pair of formals for the Avishkar interviews (Tech fest of college),a party-wear dress for Swagat(Fresher’s party).
